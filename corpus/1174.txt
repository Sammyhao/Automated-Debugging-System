 Sorting Algorithms  A Sorting Algorithm is used to rearrange a given array or list elements according to a comparison operator on the elements. The comparison operator is used to decide the new order of element in the respective data structure.  For example: The below list of characters is sorted in increasing order of their ASCII values.
